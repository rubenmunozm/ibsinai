Antioquía

We are a group of people who want to learn, love and enjoy in Christ Jesus. We want to be a friendly church where you and your family may receive love and direction from Jesus, our Lord and Savior.

We are a church that comes together to give honor and glory to God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit, through the teaching of the Bible verse by verse.We rejoice in worshiping God through music and testimonies.

Testimonies of gratitude of the blessings from God to the families in church. We are a church that promotes Christian fellowship and harmony among believers, worshiping God through loving one another and intercession through prayer.With joy we hope that you can visit us; and even more that you wish to be part of the Antioquía Family.

Calvary Chapel

In 1965, Pastor Chuck Smith began his ministry at Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa with just twenty-five people. From the beginning, Pastor Chuck welcomed all, young and old, without judgment, placing his emphasis on the teaching of the Word of God. His simple, yet sound, expositional biblical approach has helped millions better understand God’s Word.

With a sincere concern for the lost, Pastor Chuck and Kay Smith made room in the church and their home for a generation of hippies and surfers; generating a movement of the Holy Spirit that spread from the West Coast to the East Coast, and now, throughout the world.

What began as a small local church has now grown into an international ministry of over 1600 fellowships throughout the world, all in the last 40 years.

Rubén Muñoz García

With more than two decades of ministry behind him, God has allowed Ruben Muñoz to serve for his Kingdom as a pastor since October 1994, through the planting of three churches, and through the mobilization of missionaries serving as the Sonora regional office director for an international missionary agency. Currently he is the pastor of Antioquia Calvary Chapel, church God gave him the opportunity and privilege to found and serve since February 2004.

Trinity- We believe that there is one living and true GOD, eternally existing in three persons: The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, equal in power and glory. We believe that the Trinity created all, upholds all, and governs all things.

God the Father- We believe in the person of God the Father—an infinite, eternal, personal Spirit—perfect in holiness, wisdom, power, and love. We believe He concerns Himself mercifully in the affairs of men, hears and answers prayer, and saves from sin and death all those who come to Him through faith in Jesus Christ.

Jesus Christ- We believe in the person of Jesus Christ, God’s only begotten Son, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit. We believe in His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, teachings, substitutionary atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension into heaven, perpetual intercession for His people, and personal, visible return to earth.

Second Coming- We believe the Second Coming of Jesus Christ will be a personal, visible return to earth. The Second Coming will coincide with the establishment of His millennial kingdom, the resurrection, final judgment, eternal blessing of the righteous, and eternal condemnation of the wicked.

God the Holy Spirit- We believe in the person of the Holy Spirit—that He proceeds forth from the Father and Son to convict the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment; that He does regenerate, sanctify, and empower for ministry all who believe the biblical gospel.

Bible- We believe that the Scriptures, which consist of the Old and New Testaments, are the fully inspired Word of God without error in the original manuscripts, and the infallible rule of faith and practice. The Word of God is the foundation upon which the church operates, and the basis by which the church is governed. We believe that the 66 books of the Bible supersede any and all earthly law in precept and principle.
