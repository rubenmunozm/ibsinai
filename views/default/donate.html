THANK YOU FOR YOUR SUPPORT

When God gave us the vision to found Antioquia Calvary Chapel church, He showed us the need to take his word to the ends of the earth, no matter what the size of the church is, but to the God-given vision we have.

Conscious of the need to share the word of God, as the word of hope to the nations so as to the families in Hermosillo, we have financially supported various missionaries. From within our church, a brother served for two years with a missionary ministry travelling through 30 different countries bringing practical aid and word of hope; supported financially by the church.

We are aware that in order to serve in the ministries of the church, an economy that allows to cover both, the expenses of the church and those from the ministries, is necessary. We are fully convinced that God is our provider and that He uses His children to fulfill the vision that he gave.

If you decide to support, we are grateful to God and of course to you, in the understanding that we are currently focused on the "CASA ABIERTA" community center. In Casa Abierta it is necessary to take the word of hope, but also to provide of material blessings to the people that is getting close to God, therefore we encourage you to participate with your support upon this ministry.
