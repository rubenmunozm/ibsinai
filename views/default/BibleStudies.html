Bible Study Groups @ Home


"All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness." 2 Timothy 3:16

The Bible Study Groups are run by ladies and for ladies, which offer the possibility of studying the Bible seriously and systematically, allowing you to know Jesus more. There you can find women who already have found Jesus as their Lord and Savior, and who have opened their homes to welcome you with joy and are willing to take you to an encounter with Jesus. We believe that one of the greatest needs of mankind and especially the people of God, has to do with the knowledge of His Word. The Bible Study Groups offer the possibility to study the Bible in a fraternal atmosphere, where you can express and find the correct answer to any concerns you have about your spiritual life.


We are sure that you joining one of the groups, is no accident but part of God's plan. Rest assured that you are welcome in one of the houses where we study the Bible, because it is a pleasure for us to receive you.
